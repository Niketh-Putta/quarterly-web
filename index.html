The new MTD law explained clearly

Digital records required

From April 2026, affected UK sole traders must keep digital income and expense records (no paper-only tracking).

Quarterly submissions

Instead of filing once per year, updates must be submitted every 3 months (4 times per year).

Income thresholds

• April 2026 → applies if income over £50,000

• April 2027 → applies if income over £30,000
